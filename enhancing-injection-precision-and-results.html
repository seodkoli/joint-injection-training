About joint injection

In medicine, a joint shot (intra-articular injection) is a treatment used in the therapy of inflammatory joint problems, such as rheumatoid arthritis, psoriatic arthritis, gout arthritis, tendinitis, bursitis, Repetitive strain injury, and sometimes osteo arthritis. A hypodermic needle is infused right into the affected joint where it delivers a dose of any one of many anti-inflammatory agents, one of the most usual of which are corticosteroids. Hyaluronic acid, because of its high thickness, is in some cases made use of to change bursa fluids. The strategy may be made use of to likewise take out excess liquid from the joint.
